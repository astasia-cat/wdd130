History

We've been offering guided river adventures for young and old alike for 15 years. Whether you are wanting a relaxing float down a scenic river byway or an adrenaline pumping adventure through class 5 rapids, we've got you covered. Our experienced river guides are ready for any adventure you desire.

Safety is our utmost priority. All equipment is thouroughly inspected before each adventure to make sure it is in peak working order and will provide everyone with the safest experience possible. Safety equipment is provided for all of our adventurers. No matter what your skill level, an adventure awaits you!
