Overview

Purpose

Carving out our place on the internet, allowing for online scheduling of guided rafting tours. Showing that people of all ages as well as all skill levels can enjoy a rafting adventure!

Audience

1. Who are the target customers?

  • Individuals
  • Families
  • Coworkers
  • Experienced Adventurers
  • Everyone!
2. What are they looking for?
  • Individuals: People looking for a scenic relaxing float down the river. More interested in the views than the thrills and adrenaline rush of white water rapids.
  • Families: An activity that people of all ages can enjoy. Teaching water safety is the main focus while also enjoying an amazing experience.
  • Coworkers: Focusing on showing how everyone in the raft needs to work together to overcome big challenges and conquer the rapids, i.e. a team building actvity.
  • Experienced Adventurers: For those who enjoy a thrill, this will be a journey for those more familiar with rafting who have some prior experience. The guide is more a navigator on this experience, rather than teaching about rafting and water safety. These thrill seekers would get to experience the rougher rapids of the rivers!
  • Everyone!: Whatever type of experience you want to have, we can make it happen!
3. How will my audience be viewing my website?
  • Desktop
  • Laptop
  • Tablet
  • Mobile Devices
